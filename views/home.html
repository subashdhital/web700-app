Welcome to my website, I will be demonstrating HTML5 principles and techniques, Routing in Express

Information about the website

This site consists of 3 static views (Home, About, HTML Demo) as well as 5 routes that supply data, including: All Students, Students by Course, A Single Student By Student Number, All TAs, and All Courses.
For example, you can see a random student by visiting /student/2 or view students of a specific course with /students?course=3.

Intro to HTML5

This site utilizes HTML5: a markup language used for structuring and presenting content on the World Wide Web"
